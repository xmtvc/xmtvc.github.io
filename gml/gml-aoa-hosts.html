Ginny Marvin

Ads Product Liaison
Google's Ads Product Liaison, Ginny Marvin acts as a bridge between marketers and Google's ads teams. She engages with the advertising community to answer questions about how ads products and policies work and brings marketers' insights back into Google. Prior to joining Google, Ginny was Editor-in-Chief of Search Engine Land where she covered digital advertising news and trends for seven years. She got her start in digital marketing in 2005 and held in-house and agency management positions. You can find her on X and Threads at @AdsLiaison.
